PREDICTIVE MAINTENANCE TRIGGERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Based on accumulated telemetry data, the following patterns indicate
upcoming maintenance needs:

1. FUEL SYSTEM ALERT
   Trigger: fuel_rate > baseline * 1.2 for 3+ consecutive hours
   Likely cause: Injector degradation, fuel filter clog, air leak
   Action: Schedule diagnostic within 48 hours
   Historical accuracy: 85%

2. BRAKE SYSTEM ALERT
   Trigger: Speed variance consistently high + stopping distance increase
   Likely cause: Brake pad wear, fluid degradation, caliper sticking
   Action: Immediate inspection
   Historical accuracy: 78%

3. COOLING SYSTEM ALERT
   Trigger: Operating temp trend increasing > 2 degC/month
   Likely cause: Coolant degradation, thermostat failure, fan clutch
   Action: Cooling system service
   Historical accuracy: 72%

4. ENGINE DEGRADATION
   Trigger: Power loss + fuel increase + erratic idle
   Likely cause: EGR carbon buildup, DPF saturation, turbo wear
   Action: Major engine service
   Historical accuracy: 80%

5. TIRE MAINTENANCE
   Trigger: Fuel consumption increase 5-8% + GPS track variation
   Likely cause: Under-inflation, misalignment, uneven wear
   Action: Tire and alignment service
   Historical accuracy: 90%
